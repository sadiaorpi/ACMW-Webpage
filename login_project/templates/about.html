About ACM-W at Rowan University

Welcome to the Rowan University chapter of ACM-W

We are dedicated to advancing the role of women in technology fields. We believe in the power of diversity and inclusion and are committed to creating an inclusive environment where all individuals, regardless of their gender, can thrive and make a meaningful impact in the world of computing.

Our Mission

At ACM-W Rowan, our mission is to promote, celebrate, and support women in technology-related fields. We are passionate about:

  • Empowerment: We empower women to pursue their interests in technology and provide the resources and support needed to excel in their studies and careers.
  • Community: We foster a sense of community and belonging by organizing events, workshops, and networking opportunities that connect like-minded individuals and promote collaboration.
  • Education: We strive to educate our members and the broader community on the importance of diversity in technology and its benefits to innovation and problem-solving.
  • Advocacy: We advocate for gender equality in the tech industry, both within Rowan University and the broader tech community.

What We Do

ACM-W at Rowan University is a dynamic and inclusive club. We offer a range of activities and opportunities, including:

  • Speaker Series: We host inspiring speakers from the tech industry, academia, and the community, providing valuable insights and perspectives.
  • Workshops: We organize workshops on various technical and professional development topics to enhance our members' skills and knowledge.
  • Mentorship: Our mentorship program connects students with experienced professionals who can provide guidance and support in their tech journey.
  • Community Outreach: We engage in community outreach activities, promoting the importance of STEM education and encouraging underrepresented groups to pursue tech careers.
  • Networking Events: We host networking events to help students build connections with peers, faculty, and industry professionals.
  • Advocacy Initiatives: We actively work to create a more equitable tech environment, both within our university and the wider tech community.

Join Us

No matter your background or experience level, if you are passionate about technology and gender equality, ACM-W at Rowan University welcomes you with open arms. Join us in our mission to make a difference, build lasting connections, and create a brighter future for women in tech.

Together, we can shape a more inclusive and diverse tech industry. Join ACM-W at Rowan University today and be a part of the change.

Empower. Educate. Elevate.
